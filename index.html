Dish 1

Grilled Salmon
Description:

  • Our Grilled Salmon is perfectly cooked with a crispy golden-brown crust on the outside, while maintaining a tender and juicy interior. Served with a side of sautéed seasonal vegetables and a tangy lemon butter sauce, this dish offers a refreshing and flavorful experience for seafood lovers.
    • Why It's Special:
  • The salmon is sourced fresh from sustainable fisheries, ensuring both quality and environmental responsibility. Paired with a light and healthy vegetable medley, it’s the perfect choice for a nutritious and satisfying meal

    • Dish 2

    Spaghetti Carbonara
    Description:

  • A classic Italian favorite, our Spaghetti Carbonara combines perfectly al dente pasta with a creamy sauce made from rich eggs, Parmesan cheese, crispy pancetta, and freshly cracked black pepper. This indulgent dish is comfort food at its finest, served with a garnish of fresh parsley.
    • Why It's Special:
  • We prepare the carbonara the traditional Roman way, ensuring a velvety smooth sauce that clings to each strand of pasta without being too heavy. The pancetta adds a savory, slightly smoky flavor, making each bite irresistible.

    • Dish 3

    Vegetarian Salad
    Description:

  • Our Vegetarian Salad is a colorful mix of fresh greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, bell peppers, and roasted seasonal vegetables, topped with a tangy vinaigrette dressing. This light and refreshing dish is both nourishing and delicious, offering a balance of textures and flavors.
    • Why It's Special:
  • The ingredients are sourced locally, ensuring peak freshness and flavor. The vinaigrette dressing is made in-house using organic olive oil and fresh herbs, providing a burst of flavor that complements the vibrant vegetables. This is a perfect option for those seeking a healthy, plant-based meal.
